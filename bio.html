Brief Biography

Intro

Shivam Bharadwaj is a CSE student persuing B.Tech from GL Bajaj institute of technology, Greater Noida(U.P.). He lives in Bulandshahr with his mother, father, brother and sister.

History

Shivam Bharadwaj was born on 20-march-1996 in Bulandshahr(a city in Uttar Pradesh), and thus by birth is an Indian.His parents then moved to Gandhinagar(in Gujrat) due to service of his father.Due to transfers of his father in various cities he lived in various parts of country as a child, including Gandhinagar, Pune, Gurgaon, Meerut in chronological order. His family then moved to Bulandshahr where they then had a permanent address(a newly built house).He did his 10th(high school) from Renaissance School(situated in Bulandshahr) in 2011 and 12th from same school in 2013.He used to be an average student in school-time and even now.Computer Science as a subject used to fascinate him. He got his Personal Computer in 11th standard.

Currently he is persuing B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from G.L. Bajaj Institute of Technology, Greater Noida.
